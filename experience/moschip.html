🚀 My Interview Experience at MOSCHIP – SDE Technical Intern Role

📍 Campus Placement | Location: Pune / Ahmedabad | Stipend: ₹9000 + PPO Opportunity

MOSCHIP was the first company that visited our campus for placement. It offered a ₹9000 stipend with the possibility of a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO). The available roles were divided into technical and non-technical positions, with locations in Pune and Ahmedabad.

📝 Assessment Round

I was shortlisted for the assessment round and appeared for it. After two days, the results were declared — I was among the students selected for the interview process.

From the entire batch, 50 students were shortlisted for the interview. Based on roles, we were divided into respective categories. I appeared for the technical interview.

💼 Interview Experience

The interview was more than moderate in terms of difficulty. Here's what was covered:

  • ✅ Data Structures: General questions and usage scenarios
  • ✅ C Programming: Focused on functions like malloc() and calloc(), and where these are used in memory management
  • ✅ Project Discussion: I was asked to explain and draw the architecture of my mobile application development project

I successfully explained my app’s structure and UI flow, and the interviewers seemed happy with my clarity and presentation. They appreciated my diagram and approach.

🎓 Academic Question

Finally, I was asked about a subject from my last semester. Initially, I panicked and couldn't recall it for about 5 minutes, but later I remembered and gave the correct answer.

🚫 Outcome

Despite performing decently and impressing the panel with my project explanation, I was not selected in the final list. However, it was a valuable learning experience.

💡 Takeaway

This experience taught me the importance of being calm under pressure and being well-prepared with academic subjects. I also realized how crucial it is to explain projects with clarity and confidence — something that worked in my favor during this interview.

Tip for others: Practice explaining your projects and brush up on both coding and academic subjects. Always stay confident and composed, even when you momentarily forget something.
