📡 My Interview Experience at 6D Technologies – Campus Placement

📍 Online Interview | Company HQ: Bangalore | Domain: Telecom Product Development

6D Technologies, a telecommunications product company headquartered in Bangalore, visited our college for a campus recruitment drive. I was shortlisted based on my resume and invited for the interview process.

💻 Round 1: Online Technical Interview

This round was conducted online and involved a mix of coding and theoretical questions. The questions were mainly basic to medium level. Here's what they asked:

  • 🧮 Coding questions: Basic level
  • 🧠 C Programming:
    • What is if-else?
    • How does switch-case work?
    • Usage of break and continue
    • What is a static keyword?
    • What is a friend function?
  • 💡 OOP Concepts: Basic definitions and examples
  • 🗄️ MySQL:
    • Difference between DDL and DML

📌 Final Thoughts

The overall interview was quite balanced — not too hard, and most questions were based on fundamentals. A good understanding of C, OOP, and basic SQL is sufficient for cracking this kind of interview.
