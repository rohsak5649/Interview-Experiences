💼 My Interview Experience at Siemens – Campus Placement

📍 Virtual Assessment + In-Person Interview | College Campus Drive

Siemens visited our college for a campus placement drive. The process started with a proctored online assessment, followed by a detailed technical interview.

📝 Round 1: Online Assessment

The assessment was conducted on a multi-browser platform and could be taken from home or college. I appeared from home, and after successfully clearing it, I was shortlisted for the interview.

Only 8 students from my college made it to the interview round.

💻 Round 2: Technical Interview

The interview was taken by a female software engineer and lasted for almost 1 hour. The focus of the interview was mostly on my project and object-oriented programming concepts.

Key topics discussed:

  • 🎨 Figma and UI/UX fundamentals (based on my project)
  • 📱 Swift and SwiftUI
  • 🗃️ SQLite and MySQL – including SQL query writing
  • 🧠 Object-Oriented Programming – deep conceptual questions

The interviewer was especially focused on OOP concepts and tested my knowledge in great detail. I was able to answer about 80% of the questions confidently.

📌 Final Status

Despite performing well and expecting a positive outcome, my name wasn’t in the final selection list. This was disappointing, but the interview itself was one of the best learning experiences I’ve had.

💡 Key Takeaway

This interview taught me that:

  • 🧩 OOP and SQL cover nearly 70% of technical questions in such interviews.
  • 🎯 Even if the result is not in your favor, every experience adds value to your preparation.

My Advice: For companies like Siemens, focus deeply on Object-Oriented Programming and SQL queries — they play a critical role in your evaluation.
