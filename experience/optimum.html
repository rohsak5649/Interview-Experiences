📋 My Interview Experience at Optimum Financial Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

📍 Campus Placement | DY Patil Institute of Technology, Pimpri

Optimum Financial Solutions Pvt. Ltd. visited our college for a campus placement drive. My resume was shortlisted by the Training & Placement (T&P) department, and I was selected for the offline interview conducted at the college itself.

🧠 Interview Process

The interview was focused primarily on fundamental programming and MySQL concepts. Here are the key areas covered:

  • MySQL Queries: Including JOINs and SWAP logic
  • Difference between let and var in JavaScript
  • What is static keyword and its purpose
  • Write Code on Paper:
    • Reverse a string
    • Use of for loop
    • Print multiplication table of 5

📈 Result Status

The final result of the interview is still pending. However, the questions asked were basic and straightforward, so I am hopeful about the outcome.

💡 Takeaway

This experience highlighted the importance of practicing core logic building, basic SQL queries, and pen-paper coding. These are common in finance and service-based company interviews.
