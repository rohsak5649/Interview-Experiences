🚀 My Interview Experience at Accenture – Campus Placement

📍 Campus Drive | College-Based Assessment | Ongoing Status

Accenture visited our college for a campus placement drive. I was shortlisted for the initial assessment, which was conducted in our college’s computer lab.

📝 Assessment Round

The assessment included multiple sections such as aptitude, logical reasoning, and verbal ability. After clearing these sections, I was shortlisted for the next round — coding.

💻 Coding Round

In the coding round, there were two questions:

  • 🔹 One medium-level coding question
  • 🔹 One hard-level coding question

I successfully solved both problems and was selected for the next round — communication round.

🗣️ Communication Round

This round focused on:

  • ✅ English fluency
  • ✅ Grammar
  • ✅ Sentence formation and listening comprehension

After clearing the communication round, I received an email to schedule my interview. I chose my slot on 11th September.

🤝 Interview Experience

The interview was mainly HR-based and went very well — I would say around 95% good. The interviewer asked me:

  • ✅ Tell me about yourself
  • ✅ What are your hobbies?
  • ✅ Tell me about your college extracurriculars
  • ✅ Any experience with college clubs or sports?
  • ✅ Your family background
  • ✅ Situational and conditional-type questions

The overall experience was very smooth and friendly. I felt confident during the conversation and shared all answers genuinely.

📌 Final Status

As of now, the results are pending. Not only for me but also for all students who appeared for the interview. Hoping for a positive outcome!

💡 Takeaway

This interview helped me gain confidence in communication rounds and HR-style conversations. Whether selected or not, the experience was valuable and uplifting.

Tip for others: Practice speaking clearly, stay confident, and be honest during HR interviews. Sometimes, your personality makes the strongest impression.
