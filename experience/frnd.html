🚀 My Interview Experience at FRND – iOS Developer Intern Role

Off-Campus | Second Interview Attempt | Final Year Computer Engineering Student

Recently, I had the opportunity to interview at FRND for an iOS Developer Intern role. This was my second interview after facing rejection from Jar, and it turned out to be a valuable learning experience that emphasized the importance of consistent practice and deep exploration of iOS technologies.

📋 Interview Experience

The interview was relatively easy and focused on fundamental iOS concepts. Topics covered:

  • ✅ Swift concepts
  • ✅ MVVM architecture
  • ✅ State & Binding in SwiftUI
  • ✅ CoreData
  • ✅ My previous iOS internship project

⚠️ However, due to limited advanced practice, I felt I could’ve done better.

🎯 Key Takeaway

At the end of the interview, I asked the interviewer:

💡 "What are the essential skills for an iOS Developer Intern or Fresher?"

Their advice was simple yet powerful:

  • 🔹 Maintain consistency in coding
  • 🔹 Practice daily on Xcode Playground
  • 🔹 Learn a new concept every day and implement it

This experience reinforced the value of continuous learning. Although I didn’t secure the offer, I gained insights that will shape my preparation going forward.

💡💻 If you're preparing for iOS roles, remember: Consistency is key. Let's grow together!
