🎯 My Interview Experience at Revature – Training & Placement Program

📍 Online Process | Training & Client-Based Projects | Self-Development Program

I recently attended the interview process at Revature, a company that offers training and development programs and connects talent with client companies for real-world projects.

🧠 Round 1: Logical & Aptitude Assessment

The first round was an online assessment focused on logical reasoning and aptitude skills. It tested analytical thinking, basic mathematics, and problem-solving speed. I successfully cleared this round.

💻 Round 2: Technical Assessment

This round tested technical knowledge related to programming fundamentals and basic computer science concepts. Clearing this round led me to the interview phase.

🗣️ HR Interview

The final interview was HR-based and revolved around my background and personality. Some of the questions included:

  • 📘 What is your educational background?
  • 💼 How many companies have you applied to or interviewed with?
  • 💪 What are your strengths?
  • 📉 What are your weaknesses?
  • 🎓 What is your CGPA?
  • 🏆 What extracurricular activities have you participated in?

The interview mainly focused on understanding my career goals and how well I would fit into Revature's training and client-based deployment model.

📌 Final Thoughts

Revature offers a unique opportunity by providing training along with placement in client companies. The process was structured and smooth, and it helped me reflect more deeply on my own strengths and direction.
