🧩 My Interview Experience at Microland – Non-Technical Role

📍 Campus Placement | Online Process | Communication Test & Interview

Microland visited our campus for placements, offering a non-technical role. The selection process consisted of multiple steps.

🧪 Step 1: Online Assessment

The first round was an online assessment conducted by the company. After clearing this, I moved on to the next stage.

📱 Step 2: Mobile App-Based Communication Test

Candidates were required to download a mobile application where they completed three communication-based tests. Each test was scored out of 600 marks, and it was compulsory to score above 450 to qualify for the final round. I successfully cleared this stage.

🗣️ Final Interview

The final interview was focused on assessing our suitability for the role. The interviewer informed me that the offered role was completely non-technical. They asked:

“The role is non-technical. Are you comfortable with that?”

I responded honestly, saying that my interest lies in technical and coding roles, and hence I would prefer to pursue opportunities aligned with my goals.

🎯 Conclusion

This experience helped me understand the importance of aligning opportunities with career goals. Even though I was not selected, I stayed true to my passion for coding and software development.
