🟠 My Interview Experience with Swiggy – iOS Developer Intern 📱🚀

Off-Campus | Final Year Computer Engineering Student | iOS Developer Intern

I recently had the opportunity to interview with Swiggy for an iOS Developer Intern position, and I wanted to share my experience — hoping it helps others who are preparing for similar roles.

✅ Round 1: Coding Round

The process started with a coding round focused on problem-solving using basic data structures and algorithms. After successfully clearing this, I was shortlisted for the next stage.

💻 Round 2: Technical Interview (R1)

This round was centered around iOS development topics. The interviewer asked me questions based on Swift and SwiftUI, including:

  • Swift language fundamentals
  • Views and layout in SwiftUI
  • Navigation and State management
  • MVVM architecture

✅ I confidently answered around 90% of the questions!

📊 Round 3: DSA-focused Interview

This round involved:

  • Basic DSA questions
  • Medium-level coding problems
  • Dynamic Programming (DP) challenges

🧠 I handled the easy and medium problems well, but found about 70% of the DP questions difficult to understand.

🔍 Key Takeaway

Even for an internship role, companies like Swiggy expect a solid understanding of DSA along with strong platform-specific knowledge such as iOS development.

📚 This experience reminded me to continue improving my skills in both:

  • ✅ Swift & SwiftUI
  • ✅ Data Structures & Algorithms

To all fellow iOS developers and students: Keep practicing both development and problem-solving. Both are crucial in cracking interviews at top tech companies! 💪
