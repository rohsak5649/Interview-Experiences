🚀 My Interview Experience at Vistex – Campus Placement

📍 In-Person Interview at Mumbai | College Campus Drive

Vistex visited our college for a campus recruitment drive. The process was structured with an initial MCQ-based assessment, followed by two rounds of technical interviews held at the Vistex office in Andheri West, Mumbai.

📝 Round 1: Online Assessment

The first step was a multiple-choice question (MCQ) assessment focusing on technical fundamentals. I successfully cleared this round and was shortlisted for the interview.

💻 Round 2: Technical Interview – Round 1

This round included fundamental programming and technical questions such as:

  • ✅ HTML basics
  • ✅ MySQL queries
  • ✅ String reversal
  • ✅ Array reversal and addition
  • ✅ Linear search and binary search algorithms

I was able to confidently solve all these and cleared the first technical round.

💻 Round 3: Technical Interview – Round 2

After clearing the first round, only a few candidates were shortlisted — I was one of them. The second round involved deeper computer science fundamentals:

  • 🔹 Concepts of trees and graphs (theory-based problem-solving)
  • 🔹 Binary Search Trees (BST): how they work and operations
  • 🔹 Questions on multithreading
  • 🔹 A moderate-level SQL query

I answered most of the questions well, and the interview went smoothly.

📌 Final Status

Unfortunately, I was not selected in the final list. The company followed a policy of selecting only one candidate per college. A friend of mine from the same college was selected.

💡 Takeaway

Even though I wasn’t selected, I felt confident and satisfied with my performance in both technical rounds. This experience sharpened my understanding of core topics like arrays, SQL, and data structures like trees and graphs.

Advice: For companies like Vistex, focus on writing clean logic for basic problems and also be thorough with theoretical concepts of data structures and SQL.
